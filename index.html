We're looking for a Lead Engineer to join our team! In this role, you'll play a key part in shaping both the product's evolution and the engineering team, processes and culture. In this role, you will be expected to be able to work independently while collaborating with key stakeholders and customers to drive the product development past the MVP stage.

About the Company

Quidkey is a fast-growing fintech startup with a passionate and experienced team, backed by a significant pre-seed funding and strategic investors. We enable banks to embed their payment products directly into e-commerce checkout, creating a trusted and more secure bank-branded payment experience. We are a remote-first company with team members across Europe.

We are launching our initial product with our first paying customers, and this is an opportunity to help us scale and lead it into the next phase of development.

Responsibilities:

  • Work with the CTO to build the technical roadmap and drive architectural decisions for scalability and performance.
  • Lead development efforts, maintaining and enhancing the product using best practices.
  • Optimize integrations with e-commerce platforms, financial institutions, and embedded checkouts.
  • Drive security and reliability standards by implementing industry best practices.
  • Help hire, mentor, and support engineers as we grow the team.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to the development of the software architecture.
  • Collaborate with leadership to align tech and business goals as we scale.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of hands-on full-stack development experience, ideally with a significant focus on Node.js and TypeScript.
  • Experience scaling a product from MVP to high-traffic production.
  • Experience with web frameworks like Express.js, Nest.js, or Fastify.js.
  • Strong knowledge of DB technologies - SQL/NoSQL; familiarity with SQL and ORM/ODM software.
  • Knowledge of front-end JavaScript frameworks like React, Vue or Angular.
  • Experience with cloud infrastructure like GCP, AWS or Azure.
  • Familiarity with e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce) and financial APIs/Open Banking is a plus.
  • Experience leading projects or teams.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Experience with security driven development (SSDLC) and security compliance frameworks such as SOC2.

What will you be working with:

We work with Express, TypeScript, TypeORM, Sequelize, React, RESTful APIs, PostgreSQL, and GCP. If you have experience with AWS or other cloud platforms, don't worry - our DevOps will help you through.

Recruitment process

  1. Apply

    It's ok if you don't have a CV, a link to your LinkedIn profile is absolutely fine.

  2. Initial Call

    You'll have a quick chat with the CTO to discuss your background, motivation, and fit for the role and our company culture.

  3. Technical Interview

    A technical interview including a live coding challenge.

  4. Team Interview

    A session with potential future colleagues, collaboration style, and project experiences.

  5. CEO Interview

    We know interviews are a two-way process, so we'll take the time to tell you more about the role, team and culture, and answer all your questions.

  6. Offer & Feedback

    If it's a green light from us and you, we will send you an offer. Regardless of the outcome, we will provide constructive feedback.
